About our Hotel

Our Resort THE GRAND LEGACY, spread across 4 acres with spectacular views of Mountains & lush greenery, is located at the highest altitude in Mahabaleshwar. The Grand Legacy resort is the only Luxury Resorts In Mahabaleshwar with Sunset & Sunrise point. While driving from Pune, THE GRAND LEGACY is situated 6 kms before the main town and 3.5 km before the Lake.

At The Grand Legacy Resort & Spa you have different types of accommodation options to choose from Luxurious & Spacious Rooms, Suites, some with Private Swimming Pools, Ultra modern Wooden Domicile & Luxury Tents Rooms with spectacular views of lawns & mountains. All units are well appointed with classic interiors and amenities, to provide you the most pleasant experience in this hill station. Once you are here, we take care of you and your family all day long with various entertainment.
